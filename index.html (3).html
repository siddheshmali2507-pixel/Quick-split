Features
Everything in one place

No juggling between apps. QuickSplit handles groups, chats, expenses, and payments all in one clean interface.

⚡
Auto & Manual Splits
Split equally in one tap, or assign custom amounts — full flexibility for every situation.
💬
In-Group Chat
Every split has its own chat. No more buried WhatsApp threads about who owes what.
🔔
Daily Reminders
The host gets automatic daily nudges about pending payments. Zero awkward conversations.
👥
Dynamic Groups
Add or remove members from contacts anytime. Groups stay flexible as plans change.
📊
Settlement Summary
A clear view of who pays whom — no back-and-forth calculations, just clarity.
🗂️
Edit, Backup & Clear
Modify expenses anytime. Back up history or wipe it clean — your data, your control.
Process
From split to settled
in minutes

A straightforward flow from group creation to payment confirmation — without the friction.

01
Create a split
Name it "Goa Trip", "Dinner", or "Rent" and add members straight from your contacts.
02
Add expenses
Log who paid, assign participants, and pick equal or custom split mode. Edit anytime.
03
Request payment
Members get a fixed-amount request on their preferred UPI app — no app-switching needed.
04
Done — everyone's notified
Once paid, the host is notified instantly. History stays organised and backed up.
